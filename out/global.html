CapacityBanner(sig, parentElementopt) → {HTMLElement}

The `CapacityBanner` function creates a banner that displays various signal parameters and calculations related to capacity and efficiency, such as Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR), information capacity, spectral efficiency, link margin, and actual data rate.
